Me + More

Camp

Ever since attending First Year Camp as a campee in 2019, I have gone back every year and helped out as both a camp leader and organiser. I honestly think that it can be one of the funnest experiences you

can have going into university as you meet many like minded people that you are probably gonna see around for the next three years or so and you can actually make some really long lasting friendships.

Peer Mentoring

Much like first year camp, peer mentoring is something you definitely should do as you come into university cause it really helps ease the transition from high school to university. It is different as it is a much more

interactive experience and you have more time to develop lasting bonds. Asides from that, you get a constant stream of support from both experienced mentors and peers going through the same thing.
